# KrushiMedha
🌾 KrsiMedha - AI-powered multilingual farming assistant with 3-tier support system (AI → Expert → Human Call). Disease detection, fertilizer recommendations, crop analytics &amp; emergency expert access for Indian farmers.

🤖Project Overview:
KrsiMedha is an AI-powered multilingual farming assistant designed for Indian farmers. India has 120 million farmers but only 12,000 agriculture experts, creating a ratio of 1 expert for every 10,000 farmers. This gap results in 30-40% annual crop loss worth ₹2,00,000 crore and over 10,000 farmer suicides every year. Additionally, 60% of rural farmers have no reliable internet connection, 95% prefer local languages over English, and 40% are illiterate. KrsiMedha solves this by putting an AI agriculture expert in every farmer's pocket - working offline, speaking 6 Indian languages, and connecting to real human experts for critical cases.
